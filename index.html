Meetups

I'm the cofounder of two tech-related meetups. I love to hang out with interesting people and organizing something is a cool excuse to do it.

Hacks Hackers is an international organization of media tinkerers and developers, that mixes journalism and technology. Two colleagues and I started the Barcelona chapter after meeting in the CCCB data journalism sessions.

Barcelona Free Software is a monthly talk for free software geeks with 400+ members, organized by the people of KDE Spain after seeing a lack on the local scene. We already hosted people that works for Tor, the UN or MusicBrainz.
